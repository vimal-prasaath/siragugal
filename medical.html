Provision of Relief materials to the Delta regions affected by Gaja cyclone. The places assisted include Kaadanthethi, Oradiyam, Perumal Kovil Street, Thalainayar, Oralayampalam and Manakkudi of Vedaranyam TK, Nagapattinam district, Tamilnadu.
Siragugal communicated through one of the volunteers directly with the people in areas affected by Gaja cyclone and prepared a list of items which were of immediate need for the people. At Chennai, Clothes and relief materials were collected by Siragugal volunteers and Live in memories team. All the collected materials were transported to Karur through travels who were glad to help as it was for the people who were affected by Gaja. Volunteers from Siragugal and Live in memories started towards Karur on Nov 22, Friday at nightfall.

In Karur, with the help of Live in memories team, we managed to purchase all the necessary relief materials on 23rd of November. The most wonderful thing was every single one of the vendors came forward to provide the help that they could do.
