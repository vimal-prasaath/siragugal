    WHO WE ARE?

    Siragugal Trust is a registered Public Charitable Trust with recognition of Reg. No. 8/2018 registered at Manamadurai SRO, Sivagangai District, Tamilnadu.

    The Idea of starting a Trust came into existence by a common discussion among a set of friends from Coimbatore Institute Of Technology graduated in the period of 2013 - 2017. The peer discussion ended with a conclusion that something has to be done to the students studying in our institution belonging to economically weaker section.

    As a result, We, A Team Of Engineering Graduates from Coimbatore Institute Of Technology (2013-2017) stood up to raise our voice against all kinds of social issues. We don't prefer to entail us within a specific area, rather we prefer to work anywhere depending on the need and support.

    Though the People Of Siragugal Trust engaged themselves in various professions for their living, they are equally concerned towards the trust as well.

    Important thing one need to understand is that this trust was initially started aimed at helping the necessitous students of Coimbatore Institute Of Technology but we never try to limit ourselves within that particular framework. We are ready to support and voice out against any Social upheavals and focus on all facets of society which demands attention.

    WHAT WE DO ?

    The Objectives starts with the Process Of Identification. It plays a remarkable role in planting changes in the society. Though at times we argue that not everything can be obtained through money, but the acceptable fact is that money is an inevitable key to fulfill our needs.

    The process of identification involves collecting a detailed report after visiting the concerned place in person and We also check the credibility of the problem. And then it is conveyed to our board for their suggestion and approval. Finally we take up an issue or assignment only after thorough discussions considering various aspects of the issue. We are passionate about the work we do and utmost care and diligence will be contributed from every one of us to complete the work in the stipulated time.

    Fundraising is the significant part that follows identification. As you all know fund is a key ingredient in any process. We entirely depend on the people who are part of the Siragugal, and the distant friends who are generous enough to facilitate us in this noble cause. We cordially invite all who are reading this or who have the deepest spirit to serve the society to join us and lend us your support either through your funds or through your valuable participation.

    As a final step, we, The Board Of Siragugal pledge you that we will spend the wholesome of money raised only for the cause identified without leaving behind even a single penny.

    Hereby we stress that we maintain a transparent report of the money spent and anyone who are associated with the trust can approach us seeking information. And also we reaffirm that we take a decision only after due consultation with all our team members and we provide them full authority to indicate our mistakes, in case any.
